My first page about Minions

Minions are fictional characters that appear in the Despicable Me franchise, which started with Despicable Me (2010). They are characterized by their childlike behavior and unique language, which is almost intelligible at times. They are also the official mascots of Illumination, a division of Universal Pictures, which produces the films.

Kevin, Stuart, and Bob are three of the most familiar minions, who appear as stars in the film Minions (2015). Many other Minions are mentioned by name in the films and other media in the franchise. They also made a cameo appearance in the series finale of Series 15 of Ant & Dec's Saturday Night Takeaway.
